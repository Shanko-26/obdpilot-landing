When you write in, please include:

  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About → Software Version)

  • What actually happened
  • Screenshots if relevant

Frequently asked questions

My adapter isn't being detected — what do I do?

  • Make sure the adapter is plugged in and the ignition is on.
  • For Bluetooth: pair through OBD Pilot's "Connect to your car" flow, not iOS Settings. ELM327 adapters don't appear in iOS's general Bluetooth list.
  • For WiFi: join the adapter's network in iOS Settings → Wi-Fi first (often "WiFi_OBDII"), then open OBD Pilot.
  • iOS will probably say the adapter network has "no internet" — that's normal for OBD2 adapters and doesn't break anything.

Which OBD2 adapters work with OBD Pilot?

ELM327-based adapters made for cars manufactured after 1996. Both Bluetooth and WiFi variants are supported. Generic adapters sold under names like "OBDII", "Veepeak", "Vgate iCar", and "WiFi_OBDII" have all been tested.

Is OBD Pilot a replacement for a mechanic?

No. OBD Pilot is informational only. Always confirm any finding with a qualified mechanic before making a repair decision.

What's the difference between Pro and Bring Your Own Key?

  • Pro is a subscription (monthly or annual). You don't need to set up anything — chat and AI analysis just work.
  • Bring Your Own Key lets you paste your own OpenAI API key in Settings → OpenAI Key. You're then billed by OpenAI directly for usage. This is hidden by default; switch on "Use my own API key" in Settings to reveal it.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → OBD Pilot → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Why does the AI sometimes give wrong-sounding advice?

AI models can occasionally make mistakes, especially with vehicle-specific details. The trouble codes themselves are accurate (they come straight from your car's ECU); the AI's interpretation is best-effort. Confirm with a mechanic before any repair.

Does OBD Pilot work without an internet connection?

  • Demo mode and basic scan + DTC database lookups work offline.
  • The AI chat and AI analysis features need internet.

My subscription isn't showing as active — what now?

In Settings, tap "Restore Purchases" inside the paywall sheet. If it still doesn't appear, email hello@obdpilot.com with your Apple ID purchase email and the date you subscribed.

Where is my data stored?

On your device only — diagnostic scan history, chat sessions, app preferences. Your OpenAI API key (if you provided one) is stored in the iOS Keychain. Chat requests are sent to OpenAI for processing but not stored on our servers.

How do I delete my data?

Delete the app from your iPhone — all local data is removed. To revoke your OpenAI API key, do that on OpenAI's dashboard.
